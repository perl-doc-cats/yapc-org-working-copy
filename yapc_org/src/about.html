About Perl Conferences

Getting Your Event Listed Here

If you're holding a Perl event, we'd love to list it here on the Perl Conferences site. The main page events are fed from The Perl Review Perl Community Events Calendar in Google calendar. To get your event added, send email to tpf-conferences -at- perl -dot- org or contact The Perl Review.
